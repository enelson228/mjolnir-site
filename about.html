ABOUT ME

Not a corporation. Not a brand. Just a builder with a lab mindset. I like clean interfaces, hardened systems, and plans that survive contact with reality.

ORIGIN

Welcome to nellysupreme.cloud — a small slice of the internet that’s treated like a lab bench. This place exists for one purpose: to build, test, and harden. The theme is a nod to two obsessions: neon diagnostics (cyber lab) and the idea of armor as systems design (Halo).

I’m the operator behind the visor — the person who reads the logs, follows the packet trail, and refuses to ship anything I can’t observe. I like systems that fail loudly, recover cleanly, and leave receipts.

When I’m not tinkering, I’m usually doing one of three things:
• turning “it should work” into it does work
• building automation so future-me doesn’t suffer
• learning how attackers think so defenses get smarter
